What is Proxy Smart?

Proxy Smart is a smart proximity alert system designed to prevent theft and loss of personal belongings using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and real-time alerts.

Key Features

  • ✅ Real-Time Alerts: when your belongings move out of range
  • ✅ Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE): for efficient tracking
  • ✅ Secure Encrypted Data: to keep your information private
  • ✅ Long Battery Life: with energy-saving mode
  • ✅ Compact & Stylish Design: for convenience
